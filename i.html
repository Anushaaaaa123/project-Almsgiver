About

So this website basically tells us about the idea of donating food to the destitute. This website gives us a gist of all the various links and location details regarding the nearby Ophanages,Charity trusts,Temple foundations,Old age home to which the food can be voluntarily donated by any individual viewing the website when the food is overcooked at home to prevent the wastage of food.Also the food can be donated by people who cook in a large sum exclusively for donation to the trusts.The donation must be initiated by each individual during their free hours of daily schedule and no agencies would be delivering it to the trusts,as this idea is at its initial stage as of now.

In addition the same links and contact details held by the webpages in the website can also be used to donate used clothes that are well off in condition and books

Remainder:The website is created or organized only to spread humanity through the little things. We hope you lighten up your hearts and take the initiative to fill the starvation of the needy people. Our little contribution today, can change many lives tomorrow
